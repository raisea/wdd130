Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide islanders people with a great opportunity of shopping their products online from anywhere in the world

The Islander Product are the man-made products which are the handicraft.

This website will actually provide many beautiful handicrafts on online to enable the islander people purchase them

Audience

All Islanders customers will viewing thie site and they will surely love this website because they will get the product(handicrafts)

A hundred thousand of Islanders have been migrate to the US and other country. Majority of them are currently live US , and they keep calling their relatives on islands to send handicrafts but it seems so hard to get what they need because they don't see what they want . This website will of course provide photo of beautiful islander products . And all Islander Customer will access to their laptop, dekstop, mobile device and orders any islanders product they want
